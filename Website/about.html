Dune is a science fiction novel written by Frank Herbert and published in 1965. Dune takes place on the desert planet of Arrakis. Dune is the only source of the "spice" melange in the known galaxy and therefore the most important planet. The novel tells the story of the many houses and their famalies that all wish to control Dune.

It subsequently went on to become one of the most influential and greatest selling science fiction novels of all time. It has since spawned many sequels, a motion picture, television series and much more.
